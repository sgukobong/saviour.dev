## 2025-05-14 - Accessible Form Association
**Learning:** Proper association of `<label>` and `<input>` using `htmlFor` and `id` is crucial for screen readers and increases the hit area for focusing inputs, while visual `*` indicators combined with the `required` attribute provide clear multi-modal feedback about mandatory fields.
**Action:** Always ensure form fields have matching `id` and `htmlFor` attributes, and use `aria-hidden="true"` on decorative visual indicators like `*` to avoid redundant announcements.
