## 2025-05-14 - Accessibility Gaps in Custom-Styled Forms
**Learning:** Highly custom-styled forms in this application (using Tailwind 'group' patterns for layout) often prioritize visual grouping over semantic accessibility, leading to missing `htmlFor` and `id` associations.
**Action:** Always check form fields for programmatic label associations and visual 'required' indicators when working with custom Tailwind form layouts.
